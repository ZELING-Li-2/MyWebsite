I will admit I don’t volunteer much, but in high school I used to teach chess classes to children in my community. I’d stopped playing chess competitively by then, but I wanted do something useful with the ridiculous amount of chess I played as a kid. It was a fun way to introduce the kids to the game, and it was nice seeing them make new friendships over the chess board. Someday when I have free time again it would be good to do something like that again.
